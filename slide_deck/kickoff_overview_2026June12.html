Agents in the terminal

The same mental model covers Claude Code and Codex. The examples are research workflows, because that is what we do.

The one-sentence model

Chat answers.
An agent acts.

It reads your files, runs your code, looks at its own output, and tries again — until the task is done or you steer it elsewhere.

How it works

One loop, three phases.

A question may only need context. A bug fix cycles through all three, dozens of actions deep. You are part of the loop too: you can interrupt and steer at any point.
